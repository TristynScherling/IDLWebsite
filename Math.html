Math - Minecraft Rollercoaster

My roller coaster is a Minecraft recreation inspired by the American Eagle wooden coaster from Six Flags Great America. The theme centers around classic American thrill rides, with a patriotic red, white, and blue color scheme and a vintage amusement park feel. What makes it unique is that it's built entirely with a wooden structure, just like the original, capturing the towering drops, fast turns, and racing dual tracks.
